# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

## What This Covers

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it when you visit scanpick.cc, create an account, or purchase ScanPick.

**Important:** ScanPick is self-hosted software. Your warehouse data — including orders, products, workers, pick tasks, and barcode scans — stays on your own infrastructure. We never have access to it.

## What We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Name** (optional, as provided by you)
- **Email address**
- **Clerk user ID** (internal identifier)

This data is stored by [Clerk](https://clerk.com), our authentication provider. See Clerk's privacy policy at [clerk.com/privacy](https://clerk.com/privacy).

### Payment Information

When you purchase a license, payment processing is handled entirely by [Stripe](https://stripe.com). We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe shares with us:

- Payment confirmation (success/failure)
- Your email address (from the checkout form)
- The amount paid and plan purchased

See Stripe's privacy policy at [stripe.com/privacy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

### License Information

After purchase, a license record is created with [Keygen](https://keygen.sh), our license management provider. This record includes:

- Your email address
- The plan purchased (Single Site, Multi Site, Enterprise)
- Your Clerk user ID (to display the license in your account dashboard)
- Stripe session ID (for reconciliation)

See Keygen's privacy policy at [keygen.sh/privacy](https://keygen.sh/privacy).

### Email Communications

We send transactional emails via [Resend](https://resend.com):

- License delivery (after purchase)
- Renewal reminders (30, 14, and 7 days before expiry)
- Important product or account notices

See Resend's privacy policy at [resend.com/privacy](https://resend.com/privacy).

### Website Analytics

We do not use analytics or tracking scripts on scanpick.cc. No cookies are set for analytics purposes.

We may check server access logs (Cloudflare) for basic operational monitoring. These logs are retained for 7 days.

### Download Tracking

Binary downloads are hosted via GitHub Releases. GitHub may log download requests per their privacy policy at [docs.github.com/privacy](https://docs.github.com/privacy).

## What We Do NOT Collect

- **Zero warehouse data.** Orders, products, workers, pick tasks, barcodes, and all other operational data stay on your servers. We cannot see it. We do not want to see it.
- **No usage telemetry.** ScanPick makes no outbound connections for analytics, tracking, or phone-home purposes.
- **No cookies for marketing or advertising.**
- **No IP tracking beyond standard server logs.**

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for:

1. **Providing your license** — creating and managing license keys
2. **Account access** — authenticating you and displaying your licenses
3. **Communication** — sending your license key, renewal reminders, and support responses
4. **Compliance** — maintaining records of purchases for tax and accounting purposes

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Account information | Until account deletion |
| License records | 7 years (tax compliance) |
| Email logs | 90 days |
| Server access logs | 7 days |

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability** — export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [support@scanpick.cc](mailto:support@scanpick.cc).

## Data Security

- Payment data never touches our servers (handled by Stripe directly)
- Account passwords are managed by Clerk (hashed, never stored by us)
- API communications with Stripe, Keygen, Clerk, and Resend are encrypted via HTTPS
- License keys are delivered via email and accessible in your secure account dashboard
